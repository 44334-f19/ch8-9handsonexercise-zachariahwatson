In Japan, street racing is a bit different - the style, stakes, and prizes are all overwhelming. By a stroke of luck, Sean meets Han, a local racing legend, who takes Sean under his wing. After training, Sean risks it all when he faces the local champion for the ultimate prize: the champion's girlfriend.
